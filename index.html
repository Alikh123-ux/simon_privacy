Privacy Policy for Simon - Wake Up Alarm

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Introduction

Simon ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Simon mobile application.

Information We Collect

Simon is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Alarm Settings: Your alarm times and preferences are stored locally on your device only.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, Apple processes your payment. We do not have access to your payment details.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, contact lists, location data, or usage analytics.

How We Use Information

All alarm settings and app preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that collect or store your data.

Third-Party Services

Simon uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles purchase data.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Children's Privacy

Simon is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app.
